# UCAISM Global Initiative

**ucaism.org** — Official website of the UCAISM Global Initiative

Advancing user-centric AI architecture for individual sovereignty.

## About

The UCAISM Global Initiative is the research home of **Chang-Gyu Kwag**, PhD, Adjunct Professor of Economics at Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, Seoul.

The Initiative develops and advances the UCASM (User-Centric AI Sharing Model) and UCAISM (User-Centric AI Individual Sovereignty Model) frameworks — a third way between platform capitalism and state-controlled AI.
