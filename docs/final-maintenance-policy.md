# UCAISM.org — Final Maintenance Policy

After Phase A–F, the site should no longer be treated as a build-everything development project. It should be maintained as a stable global research and author platform.

## Maintenance rhythm

Recommended rhythm:

- Monthly: check homepage, sitemap, core research pages, and contact links.
- Quarterly: update research/publication status, media resources, and citation information.
- Before public campaigns: verify social previews, Open Wins links, author bio, and media kit.
- Before adding any major feature: ask whether the feature strengthens the main institutional mission or simply adds complexity.

## Content rule

Prefer fewer, higher-quality pages over many thin pages.

## Technical rule

Avoid adding databases, heavy frameworks, tracking-heavy scripts, or complex external dependencies unless there is a clear strategic reason.

## Brand rule

Keep UCAISM.org focused on:

1. UCAISM Global Initiative
2. Open Wins
3. Intelligent Capitalism as a forthcoming intellectual architecture
4. Research, policy, and public-education resources
5. Individual sovereignty in the age of AI and platform power
