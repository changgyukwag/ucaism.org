A public forum for the New Enlightenment

The Events hub turns UCAISM.org into a convening platform for research discussions, civic education, institutional dialogue, media conversations, and global public learning.

Each event should connect current debates to deeper questions of architecture: who owns the rails, who controls the data, who governs AI, and how individuals retain standing in platform-mediated economies.

Institutional role

Events should be designed as serious public-education instruments rather than promotional announcements. The goal is to build intellectual trust, research visibility, and a global community around UCAISM.
