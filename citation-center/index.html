Why a Citation Center?

The UCAISM.org platform is designed as both an author platform and a global research initiative. A dedicated Citation Center helps readers, scholars, journalists, and policymakers cite the work consistently across academic, policy, and media contexts.

Preferred institutional name

UCAISM Global Initiative

Use this name when citing the platform, research initiative, or institutional materials. The initiative may be described as an independent research and public-education initiative founded by Chang-Gyu Kwag.

Use and consistency

Citation Guidelines

Author name

Use Chang-Gyu Kwag in English-language citations unless a specific publisher or journal style requires a different transliteration.

Institutional name

Use UCAISM Global Initiative for institutional outputs, research-center materials, and platform-level citations.

Forthcoming works

For works not yet published, use “forthcoming” or “manuscript in preparation” rather than assigning a publication date.

Versioned materials

Working papers should include version numbers when available. Policy briefs should include publication month and year when available.
