Why this library exists

From author website to research institution platform

The Research Library is designed as the intellectual backbone of UCAISM.org. It gives visitors a clear path from the book project to research papers, policy notes, working papers, and public-education resources.

Core fields

AI governance, data sovereignty, platform power, digital public infrastructure, civic technology, and the political economy of Intelligent Capitalism.

Publishing logic

Separate long-form scholarship, public-facing policy briefs, working drafts, books, media resources, and citation assets without fragmenting the brand.
